"
Describe the basic properties of peanos natural numbers:

1) 0 is a natural number
2) if n is a natural number, then suc(n) is a natural number

Given these 2 properties, can we prove suc(suc(0)) (=2) is a natural number?
"
(is_nat(_0) -> true) &
(sum n # is_nat(suc(n)) -> is_nat(n)) &
is_nat(suc(suc(_0)))
